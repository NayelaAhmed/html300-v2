Greeting card pricing information

Note: The prices are based upon expenses occurred to date and include projected future cost.

Group 1: Specialty Hand-made greeting card
Boxed Sets: Boxed sets are sold in groups of 10. There are two variations of designs. Elaborate designs set of 10 in a Box sell for $20. Simple designs set of 10 in a Box for $15. Individual Cards: Designed for individuals who pride themselves on finding just the right card for any occasion. Individual cards have multiple variations of designs for many occasions such as Mother’s Day, Father’s Day, Anniversary, Get Well, Thank You and Holidays. Simple individual cards priced at $5 each, and elaborate cards at $7 each. Size: The dimensions of these cards will be 5”x7”
Group 2: Digital printed greeting card
Boxed Sets: Boxed sets are sold in groups of 10. There are two variations of designs. Elaborate designs set of 10 in a Box sell for $15. Simple designs set of 10 in a Box for $10. Individual Cards: Designed for individuals who pride themselves on finding just the right card for any occasion. Individual cards have multiple variations of designs for many occasions such as Mother’s Day, Father’s Day, Anniversary, Get Well, Thank You and Holidays. Simple individual cards priced at $3 each, and elaborate cards at $5 each. Size: The dimensions of these cards will be 5”x7”
